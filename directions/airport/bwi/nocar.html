From Baltimore/Washington International Airport:

Traveling by Transit

Change travel method

Maps: Metrobus, Metro, Shuttle UM, Campus

Note: If you get to the Metro station from 3-7 PM, you can also take the Yellow Line directly to College Park/U of MD Metro Station. Follow the same direction as the Green Line.

  1. From the airport, head to the airport Metrobus Stop (B30).
  2. Take the Metrobus B30 (BWI Express) to Greenbelt.
  3. At Greenbelt, head to the Metro Station at the stop.
  4. Take the Metro Green Line.
  5. Get off at the College Park/U of MD Metro Station.
  6. At the College Park Metro station, take the Shuttle UM to campus.
  7. Get off at the Regents Drive Garage stop.
  8. Walk this path to arrive at the Samuel Riggs IV Alumni Center.
