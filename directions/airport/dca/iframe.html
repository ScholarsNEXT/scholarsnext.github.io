From Ronald Reagan National Airport:
(Maps: Metro, Shuttle UM, Campus) Take the Metro Yellow Line towards Fort Totten. At Fort Totten, transfer to the Metro Green Line towards Greenbelt. Get off at the College Park/U of MD Metro Station. Take the Shuttle UM to campus. Get off at the Regents Drive Garage stop. Then walk this path to arrive at the Samuel Riggs IV Alumni Center. Note: If you depart from 3-7 PM, you can simply the Yellow line directly to College Park/U of MD Metro Station.
