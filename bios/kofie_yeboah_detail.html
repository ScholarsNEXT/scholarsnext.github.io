Kofie Yeboah

Speaker

Kofie Yeboah is a junior broadcast journalism major at the University of Maryland. He is the creator of a series called Millennial Spotlight on USA TODAY College, a series that takes extraordinary millennials and profiles them on the site. He is also the co-founder of two websites: The Left Bench, an up-and-coming sports news site; and Screenranks, a new site dedicated to reviewing TV shows.
