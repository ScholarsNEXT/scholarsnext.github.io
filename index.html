about conference

ScholarsNext is a student run group that provides students the opportunity to share the real-world problem solving in their ideas and projects to the College Park Scholars and the University of Maryland. All of these projects share unconventional ways of resolving everyday problems by using a shift in thinking. Student presentations will research and explore a variety of ideas, from music therapy for the elderly, to the correlation between watercolor painting and mathematics, to the special eureka moment that defines a groundbreaking idea. They will present their speeches in a way that aims to educate, inspire, and include the audience. While all of these concepts vary in their specific points, they all share the common theme of approaching a problem from a fresh perspective.
